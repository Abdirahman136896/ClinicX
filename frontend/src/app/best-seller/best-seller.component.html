    About The Hospital

    The ClinicX is a multispecialty Hospital with branches in Kenya located in Nairobi, Mombasa and Kisumu. We offer patient-focused, world class medical treatment and accord the highest standards of medical care to all our patients indiscriminately. Since its inception in 2011 ClinicX Hospital has been acclaimed for excellence in clinical care in Africa.
